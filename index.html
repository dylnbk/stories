Out in the darkness, a twinkling star felt very lonely.

It had been shining brightly for billions of years, but it never had a friend.

So...

The star sent a wish out into space, hoping that someone or something would hear it.

As the wish spread through the cosmos, it began to take shape.

A new solar system was born, with planets and moons spinning around the majestic star.

Overjoyed by its new friends the star danced with delight, shimmering brightly as it travelled through space.

Together, the star and its newfound companions basked in light, celebrating their unique connection to one another.

As they laughed and cheered in wonder at all they had created, the star knew that this was only the beginning of an incredible journey through the vast expanse of the universe.
